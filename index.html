Repair Services

  • Washing Machines (Front-loading Only)

    I specialize on LG and Samsung machines but I have worked on all brands for the last 10 years. I can change all components on a washer including the bearings and the shaft. I charge a flat fee plus the cost of the parts so you will always know what the cost will be!

  • Dryers

    I also repair all brands of dryers. Once again, I charge a flat fee plus the cost of the parts required. Also, no matter what I fix on your dryer, I will vaccum the inside and check all components for proper function.

About me

I am a retired mechanical engineer with 30 years of experiance in the automotive industry. I have always enjoyed working on mechanical components and have felt that mechanical things are a form of art.

Therefore, it is not surprising that even patents refere to the new inventions as the "Art".

I started working on washers and dryers when my machine had a problem and I figured out how to fix it. I really enjoyed it! After that, I would fix machines for my friends and started buying machines and diagnosing them so my kids could sell the good parts on Ebay. In the process of trying to show my kids how to run a buisness, I picked up the skills to run my own.

I take on very few jobs so I can give you my undivided attention when I am working for you!
